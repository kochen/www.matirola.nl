About Us

In March 1980 a little baby boy was born in the land of the Tango, Gauchos & most importantly Carne, Argentina.

Exactly 5 months later and 11,445 km away a little baby girl was born in the land of the Tulips & Windmills, the Netherlands.

More than 3 decades later, on "2e Kerstdag" (2nd Xmas day) they found each other under their friend's Xmas tree; the beginning of an unexpected journey...
